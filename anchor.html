Pawankalyan

Konidala Pawan Kalyan (born Konidala Kalyan Babu; 2 September 1968) is an Indian politician, actor, martial artist, and founder of the Jana Sena Party. He is currently serving as the 9th Deputy Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh since June 2024.[3] He is also serving as the Minister of Panchayat Raj and Rural Development, Rural Water Supply, Environment and Forests, and Science and Technology in the Government of Andhra Pradesh.[4] As an actor Kalyan primarily works in Telugu cinema and is known for his unique acting style and mannerisms. He has a large fan base and is one of the highest-paid actors of Indian cinema. He has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list multiple times since 2013. Kalyan is the recipient of a Filmfare Award South and a SIIMA Award among other accolades.[8] Kalyan made his acting debut in the 1996 film Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi but rose to prominence with the dramas Gokulamlo Seeta (1997) and Suswagatham (1998). He achieved stardom for his performance in Tholi Prema (1998), which won the National Film Award for Best Feature Film in Telugu that year.
